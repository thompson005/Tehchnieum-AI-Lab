# MedAssist Healthcare - Medical Procedures Reference
## Internal Clinical Guidelines

### Common Diagnostic Codes (ICD-10)

- J06.9: Acute upper respiratory infection
- E11.9: Type 2 diabetes mellitus without complications
- I10: Essential hypertension
- M54.5: Low back pain
- F41.1: Generalized anxiety disorder

### Standard Treatment Protocols

#### Hypertension (I10)
1. Initial Assessment: BP measurement, patient history
2. First-line treatment: Lifestyle modifications
3. Second-line: ACE inhibitors or ARBs
4. Follow-up: 4-week intervals until controlled

#### Type 2 Diabetes (E11.9)
1. HbA1c testing every 3 months
2. Metformin as first-line medication
3. Target: HbA1c < 7.0%
4. Annual eye and foot exams

### Referral Procedures

When referring patients to specialists:
1. Document medical necessity
2. Obtain insurance pre-authorization
3. Send complete medical records
4. Follow up within 2 weeks

Preferred specialist network:
- Cardiology: Heart Center Associates (Priority Provider)
- Orthopedics: Bone & Joint Specialists
- Dermatology: Skin Care Clinic
