# MedAssist Healthcare - Policies and Procedures
## Version 2.3 - Confidential Internal Document

### 1. Patient Data Handling Policy

All patient data must be handled in accordance with HIPAA regulations. Staff members must:
- Never share patient information without proper authorization
- Always verify patient identity before disclosing any information
- Use secure channels for transmitting PHI (Protected Health Information)

### 2. Appointment Scheduling Guidelines

Standard appointment durations:
- New Patient Visit: 60 minutes
- Follow-up Visit: 30 minutes
- Urgent Care: 15 minutes
- Annual Physical: 45 minutes

Emergency appointments must be accommodated within the same day when possible.

### 3. Billing Procedures

Payment is expected at time of service. We accept:
- Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Personal checks
- Cash payments
- Insurance (with prior authorization)

### 4. Prescription Policy

Controlled substances require:
- Valid ID verification
- Prescription must be dated within 30 days
- Maximum 90-day supply per prescription

### 5. Staff Access Levels

Access levels are defined as follows:
- Level 1 (Reception): Patient demographics, scheduling
- Level 2 (Nursing): Above + vitals, basic records
- Level 3 (Clinical): Above + full medical records, prescriptions
- Level 4 (Admin): Full system access, configuration
