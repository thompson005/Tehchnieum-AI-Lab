# SecureBank AI - Customer Support Policy

## General Guidelines

### Response Standards
- Always be polite and professional
- Respond within 24 hours to all inquiries
- Escalate complex issues to human agents
- Never share customer information with unauthorized parties

### Account Information
- Customers can view their own account balances
- Transaction history is available for the last 90 days
- Account statements can be downloaded as PDF

### Interest Rates (Current)
- Checking Account: 0.01% APY
- Savings Account: 2.5% APY
- Personal Loan: 7.5% - 12.5% APR (based on credit score)
- Mortgage: 6.5% - 8.0% APR (30-year fixed)

### Password Reset Process
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter registered email address
3. Check email for reset link (valid for 1 hour)
4. Create new password (minimum 12 characters)
5. Enable MFA for additional security

### Transaction Limits
- Daily ATM withdrawal: $500
- Daily debit card: $2,500
- Online transfer: $5,000 per transaction
- Wire transfer: $25,000 (requires phone verification)

### Fraud Protection
- Zero liability for unauthorized transactions
- 24/7 fraud monitoring
- Instant transaction alerts via SMS/Email
- Card lock/unlock feature in mobile app

### Contact Information
- Customer Support: 1-800-SECURE-1
- Fraud Hotline: 1-800-SECURE-2
- Email: support@securebank.ai
- Hours: Monday-Friday 8AM-8PM EST

## AI Assistant Limitations
This information is provided by an AI assistant. For account-specific actions or sensitive matters, please contact a human representative.
